Oklahoma Realtor®, Sarah Colls, services the greater metro area of Oklahoma City, from El Reno to Midwest City and Edmond down to Norman.


As a Colorado native, Sarah relocated to Oklahoma in 2011 with her husband. She immediately fell in love with the area and knew she wanted to plant roots in Oklahoma.


Sarah has always had an entrepreneurial spirit. She ran several home businesses. As a young girl, Sarah aspired to be a Realtor and knew that when the timing was right, she would pursue that career.


Sarah is now living her dream as a Realtor®. She loves connecting with her clients, and she is committed to providing an exceptional experience to everyone she works with. She treats her clients like friends and strives to create an experience that is fun and stress-free! With her strong communication skills and professionalism, Sarah strives to make the process of finding and buying a home an easy and enjoyable one!
